Tj's Dev Showcase

Project Overview

The purpose of this application is to promote a friend's coding endeavors. He wants to be able to show off his projects and achievements to potential employers.

The intended users of the website are recruiters and potential employers looking for his projects and accomplishments.

The page will showcase coding projects, achievements, and certifications.
